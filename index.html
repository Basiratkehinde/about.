ABIOLA AKINYEMI

Abiola Akinyemi, is a British psychologist from Osun state, Nigeria. Born on the 3rd of december, 1960.

EDUCATION:

He graduated from the university of East London with a Bsc in Psychology,
He had his MA in Educational Leadership at the University of East London, United Kingdom.

CAREER:

He is a lecturer and currently pursuing further studies in Education at the University of East London, United Kingdom.

FAMILY:

He is blessed with two children and two grandchildren.

PASSION:

He is a catholic, a lover of God and a philantropist, he gives to the needy in order to create a better world.
A top fan of Donald Trump.
